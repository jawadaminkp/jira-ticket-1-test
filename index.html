About Us

Sunlight Hospitality and Tourism Services is a tourism consulting business in Peshawar. Aiming high to provide premium tourism services primarily to our own people and also the foreigners for a reliable and comfortable services. Sunlight services have Initiated premium tourism services since tourism has emerged as an important sector which can play the role of an engine for achieving sustainable economic growth in the region and also open up the region for external visitors. This increased interaction will enhance the mutual understanding between the people of Northern and settled areas which will ultimately lead to greater cooperation and social partnerships between the local population and the people residing from other regions of Pakistan.
